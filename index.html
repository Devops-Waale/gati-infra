About the Firm

Who is Gati Infra Project Buildcon Pvt Ltd?

Gati Infra Project Buildcon Pvt Ltd is a trusted execution partner in the construction and infrastructure sector, specializing in RCC structural works across Gurgaon, Delhi NCR & Haryana.

We undertake residential and commercial projects, delivering end-to-end structural execution — from foundation to top slab — with our in-house teams of shuttering, reinforcement, and site execution.

Our strength lies in speed, precision, and consistency, enabling us to handle large-scale projects while maintaining strict quality and safety standards.

We are committed to building long-term partnerships with builders, contractors, and organizations by delivering on-time, high-quality, and dependable execution services.
